About Us

Nut Craze: Color Sorting Game is an addictive puzzle experience where players sort colorful nuts into their correct color groups. With progressively challenging levels and an engaging gameplay loop, Nut Craze is designed for anyone looking for a fun and brain-stimulating game. Embark on an exciting sorting adventure and master your skills!

HOW TO PLAY

  • Sort Nuts

    Drag and drop nuts of different colors into the corresponding colored jars.

  • Match and Complete Levels

    Solve puzzles and unlock new challenges as you progress.

  • Earn Rewards

    Collect stars for completing levels and use them to unlock new features and bonuses.

  • Unlock New Jars

    As you advance, unlock more jars and get access to new sorting mechanics.

  • Boosters and Power-ups

    Use powerful boosters to help you complete tricky levels.
